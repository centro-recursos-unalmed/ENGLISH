Writing Section Directions
  This section tests your ability to communicate in writing in an academic environment. There is one writing task.

For this task, you will answer a question based on what you already know.
  Independent Writing Directions
  In this task, you will write an essay that states, explains, and supports your opinion about an issue. You have 30 minutes to plan, write, and revise your essay.

A successful essay will usually be at least 300 words. You should show that you can write well by developing your ideas, organizing your essay, and using language accurately to express your ideas.

If you finish your essay early, you can click on Next to end this section.
