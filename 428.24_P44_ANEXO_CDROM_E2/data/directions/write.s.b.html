Writing Section Directions
Independent Task
 

Take out a pencil or pen and a piece of paper for your notes.

In this section, you will read a question asking you to state, explain, and support your opinion about an issue.

Take notes for the main ideas of your response.

You can compare your notes to sample notes and read a sample answer to the question.

Then you will write answers to questions about the sample written response.
